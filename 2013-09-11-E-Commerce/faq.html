F.A.Q

1. What exactly is HalloweenShopping.com?

We are one stop shop for all your Holiday needs.

2. Do you ship to international addresses?

We currently ship to the 50 United States, Puerto Rico, APO/FPO addresses, and Canada.

3. How do I track my order?

Your order will ship by FedEx or USPS. The carrier is determined by the quickness and the location of the delivery. If the order is shipped by USPS, no tracking information will be provided. It will take 3-8 business days for your package to be delivered.

4. What is your return policy?

We do not accept returns.

5. When will my credit card be billed?

We will preauthorize your credit card when you place your order, but it won't actually get charged until we ship your order.
